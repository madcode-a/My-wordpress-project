== Description ==

Roofing Flooring is a specialized WordPress theme designed for businesses in the roofing and flooring industry. This theme is perfect for companies offering roofing services such as roof repair, roof replacement, roof installation, and roof maintenance. Itâ€™s also ideal for those providing roof inspection, roof leak repair, and roof restoration services. Additionally, this theme caters to both residential roofing and commercial roofing needs, as well as industrial roofing. One of the key benefits of this theme is its focus on showcasing a wide range of roofing services. The theme includes dedicated sections for different types of roofing, such as flat roofing, metal roofing, shingle roofing, tile roofing, slate roofing, asphalt roofing, EPDM roofing, TPO roofing, modified bitumen roofing, PVC roofing, green roofing, and solar roofing. This comprehensive approach ensures that potential clients can easily find and understand the services offered. In addition to roofing, the theme also addresses flooring needs, including floor fixing, floor maintenance, floor repair, and floor refinishing. It covers a range of flooring options such as garden flooring, paving, tiles, tiling, and floor refinishing. Visually, the Roofing Flooring WordPress Theme is stunning and professional. It features a clean and modern design, with high-quality images and a well-organized layout that highlights essential information. The theme is fully responsive, ensuring that your website looks great on all devices, from desktops to mobile phones. The retina-ready design guarantees sharp and vibrant visuals, enhancing the overall aesthetic appeal.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type in Roofing Flooring in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.
3. Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.
4. Navigate to Appearance > Customize in your admin panel and customize to taste.
